Privacy Policy for iRemove

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Overview

iRemove is designed with privacy in mind. All image processing runs entirely on your device.

Photo Processing

Photos edited in iRemove are processed locally on your device. Images are never uploaded to our servers and are not stored externally.

Data Collection

iRemove does not collect or store your photos.

Advertising

iRemove uses third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect anonymized device information, advertising identifiers, and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

For more information about how Google handles data, please review their Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

AI Training

Your images are never used for AI training.
