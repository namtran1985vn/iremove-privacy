Privacy Policy for iRemove

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Overview

iRemove is designed with privacy in mind. All image processing runs entirely on your device.

iRemove does not require account registration.

Photo Processing (On-Device)

Photos edited in iRemove are processed locally on your device. Your images are not uploaded to our servers and are not stored externally by iRemove.

Data Collection

iRemove does not collect, store, or transmit your photos.

However, iRemove integrates third-party advertising services. These third-party services may collect certain device-related information (such as advertising identifiers, usage data, and diagnostics data) to provide and improve advertising functionality, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

iRemove uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and other device or usage information to deliver and measure ads, and to help prevent fraud. iRemove does not have access to or control over the data collected by Google AdMob.

For more information, please review Google’s Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

AI Training

Your images are never used for AI training by iRemove.
